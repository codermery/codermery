## About

I am a robotics software engineer and researcher from Türkiye, working at the intersection of **robotics**, **computer vision**, and **autonomous systems**.

My work focuses on building practical AI-powered robotic systems, from simulation and model evaluation to embedded deployment and real-world testing.

Currently, I am developing research and engineering projects around:

- Multi-robot SLAM and map merging
- Autonomous driving perception
- Safety-oriented AI systems
- Warehouse robotics and AMR systems
- Embedded robotics and real-time control
